New International Student Orientation
Welcome to Aggieland!
The ISSS New International Student Orientation (NISO) is an ONLINE source of information for all incoming international students on important issues such as ISSS services, maintaining visa status, health and safety, TAMU departments and student organizations, and local community resources in Aggieland and the local community of Bryan College Station. It includes various videos and links to other websites.
We look forward to you arriving in Aggieland!
